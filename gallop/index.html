How to Play
  1. January: Draft 5 horses, 2 trainers, and 2 jockeys
  2. February: Add on 5 horses
  3. April/May: Add final 5 horses, for 15 total
  4. Earn points throughout a 14-week (25-race) schedule into the Kentucky Derby
  5. Finish in the Top Tier to qualify for prizes
