Falcons Clinch Final Playoff Spot
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online
August 29, 2005

The Falcons went out and took care of business last night against an old rival, Smith Barney. The Falcons, behind strong pitching and defense beat a tough Smith Barney squad 4-1. Highlights included Bob K's strong performance on the mound, with assistance from veteran William Mills behind the plate. The Falcon defense was solid all around, especially Keith Stanick (CF), Adam Bloom (LF) and rookie Chris Schwab (2B).

Smith Barney was able to escape a couple of Falcons threats early on, however, the Falcons still managed to put some runs across the board when they needed to. Justin Picciano stepped up with a couple of timely hits; Dan Euerle started us off with a big triple in one inning; and Adam and Chris put pressure on the Smith Barney pitcher by drawing key walks. Adam also came up with a clutch RBI double when the team needed a big hit. Follo and Rupe came up with a couple of clutch at bats, including a sac fly when the team needed some insurance...Even Todd Ames contributed by providing a nice breeze on an otherwise humid and muggy evening (the whiiiiiff)....All in all, a great team effort, and that's what Falcon ball is all about....PLAYOFF TIME IS FALCON TIME!!!
