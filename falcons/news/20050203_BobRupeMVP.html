1B Bob Rupe named 2004 MVP
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online

February 3, 2005 -- For the 2nd consecutive season, Bob Rupe was named the Falcons MVP. Bob received 8 out of the 11 votes cast by his fellow teammates.

During the 2004 Season, Bob led the Falcons in RBI (9), Hits (16), and Doubles (2). Bob also tied for the team lead in Singles (13).
