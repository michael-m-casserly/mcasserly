August 8, 2003 Rain Out Rescheduled
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online

August 8, 2003 -- The Falcons game vs. Flushing Toilets on Friday, August 8, 2003 was rained out. The game has been rescheduled for 7:00pm on Thursday, August 21, 2003 at the North Meadow Field #3. Please view the schedule section for further details.
