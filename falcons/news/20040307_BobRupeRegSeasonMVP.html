1B Bob Rupe named 2003 Regular Season MVP
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online

March 7, 2004 -- Bob Rupe was named the Falcons 2003 Regular Season MVP. Bob received 9 out of the 13 votes cast by his fellow teammates.

During the Falcons 2003 Season, Bob led the team with 2 HRs, 11 RBI, and a .933 Slugging Percentage. Bob also tied for the team lead in Doubles (2). Bob was also solid with the glove, posting a .981 fielding percentage in 10 games at first base.
