Pitcher Bob Kostulias named 2002 Post-Season MVP
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online

March 7, 2003 -- In another landslide vote, Bob Kostulias was named the Falcons 2002 Post-Season MVP. Bob received 8 out of the 11 votes cast by his fellow teammates.

During the Falcons Championship march, Bob was undefeated (4-0) notching 5 strikeouts, and posting a 2.50 ERA. The Falcons, a 4th seed underdog entering the post-season, faced the best of the best during their playoff campaign, and Bob was always up for the task. Congratulations to Bob and Falcons for an impressive 2002 Post-Season run.
