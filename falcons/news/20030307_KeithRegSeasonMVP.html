OF Keith Stanick named 2002 Regular Season MVP
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online

March 7, 2003 -- In a landslide vote, Keith Stanick was named the Falcons 2002 Regular Season MVP. Keith received 8 out of the 11 votes cast by his fellow teammates.

During the Falcons 2002 Season, Keith was truly the offensive leader, leading the team with 4 HRs, 13 RBI, and a whopping 1.029 Slugging Percentage. Keith also tied for the team lead in Runs Scored (12), and Hits (17).
