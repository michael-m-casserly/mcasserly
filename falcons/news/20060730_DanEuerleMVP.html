3B Dan Euerle named 2005 MVP
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online

July 30, 2006 -- In a landsilde vote, Falcon 3B Dan Euerle was named the Falcons 2005 MVP. Dan received 10 out of the 15 first-place votes cast by his fellow teammates.

During the 2005 Season, Dan led the Falcons in Runs Scored (12), Hits (15), Triples (2), Home Runs (2), Slugging Percentage (1.083), On-Base Percentage (.667), and Batting Average (.625)
