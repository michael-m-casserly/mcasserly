OF Aaron Scheid named 2003 Post-Season MVP
By Michael Casserly / Falcons Online

March 7, 2004 -- In a landslide vote, Aaron was named the Falcons 2003 Post-Season MVP. Aaron received 10 out of the 12 votes cast by his fellow teammates.

During the Falcons post-season run, Aaron batted .600 with a HR, 5 RBI and a whopping 1.200 Slugging Percentage. The Falcons, a 3rd seed underdog entering the post-season, were involved a couple of offensive slug-fests during their playoff run, and Aaron was the main catalyst behind that offensive outburst. Congratulations to Aaron and Falcons for a gutsy 2003 post-season performance.
