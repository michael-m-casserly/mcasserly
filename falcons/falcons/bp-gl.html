Great Lawn--Central Park & 81st Street

The Great Lawn Fields are located in Central Park on 81st Street. There are 8 ball fields, located at mid-park from 81st through 85th Streets.

Phone Number:
212-628-1036 ext. 806

Directions to the Great Lawn fields:
By Subway, take the "B" or the "C" train to the 81st Street Station (Museum of Natural History). Enter Central Park West at 81st Sreet and walk towards the middle of the park. The field numbers are located on the backstops.
