Locations And Directions To All Falcons' Ball Parks...

The Falcons play a majority of their games in Central Park, New York City. On occasion, there are games played in Riverside Park, Dewitt-Clinton Park, and Washington Heights, also located in New York City. Please visit the links below for detailed directions on how to get to each field.

Dewitt-Clinton Fields--52nd Street & 11th Avenue, NYC

Great Lawn--Central Park & 81st Street

Harlem River Drive Fields--128th Street & 3rd Avenue, NYC

Heckscher Fields--Central Park & 63rd Street

North Meadow--Central Park & 98th Street
