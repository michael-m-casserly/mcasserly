Heckscher Fields--Central Park & 63rd Street

The Heckscher Ballfields are 6 ballfields located at 63rd Street between the west and center drives.

Directions to the Heckscher fields:


By Subway, take the "A" "B" "C" "D" "1" or the "9" train to the 59th Street Station (Columbus Circle). Enter Central Park West (anywhere between 59th and 65th Street) and walk towards the middle of the park. The field numbers are located on the backstops.
