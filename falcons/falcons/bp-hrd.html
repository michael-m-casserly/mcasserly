Harlem River Drive Fields--128th Street & 3rd Avenue, NYC

The Harlem River Drive Fields are located on 128th Street and 3rd Avenue near the Harlem River Drive in New York City. The ball fields are lighted for night games.

Directions to the Harlem River Drive fields:
By Subway, take the "4" "5" or the "6" train to the 125th Street Station. This will leave you on Lexington Avenue and 125th Street. Walk north 3 blocks and east 1 block towards 128th Avenue and 3rd Avenue.
By Subway, take the "2" or the "3" train to the 125th Street Station. This will leave you on Lenox Avenue and 125th Street. Walk north 3 blocks and east 5 blocks towards 128th Avenue and 3rd Avenue.
